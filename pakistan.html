Pakistan

Pakistan or the Islamic Republic of Pakistan ( Urdu : Islami Jamhuri-e Pakistan ) is an Islamic republic located in western India . The major languages ​​here are Urdu , Punjabi , Sindhi , Balochi and Pashto . The capital is Islamabad and other important cities are Karachi and Lahore . Pakistan has four provinces: Punjab , Sindh , Baluchistan and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa , along with Pakistan-occupied Kashmir , Gilgit-Bastistan and Islamabad Capital Territory are also in Pakistan. Pakistan was born in 1947 as a result of the partition of India. The two-nation theory was first mentioned by the poet Muhammad Iqbal in 1030 . He talked about forming a new nation by merging Sindh, Baluchistan, Punjab and Afghanistan in the north-west of India. In 1933, Chaudhary Rahmat Ali, a student at Cambridge University, coined the term Pakstan (which later became Pakistan) for the people of Punjab, Sindh, Kashmir and Baluchistan . From 1947 to 1972, Pakistan was divided into two parts - East Pakistan and West Pakistan. As a result of war with India in December, 1971, East Pakistan became Bangladesh and West Pakistan remained Pakistan. Pakistan is a member of United Nations , Shanghai Cooperation Organization , Organization of Islamic Cooperation , Commonwealth of Nations , South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation, SAARC Islamic Military Alliance.
