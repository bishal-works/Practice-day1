Famous Places

Kathmandu

Kathmandu is the federal capital of Nepal and the most populous city in Nepal. This metropolis is located at an altitude of 1,400 meters in the Kathmandu Valley, which is the highest plateau of Bagmati Province and central Nepal.

Pokhara

Pokhara is a metropolitan city located in Kaski District, Gandaki Province, Nepal. V.No. On January 18, 2052, Pokhara was declared a sub-metropolitan city by the Ministry of Federal Affairs and Local Development. Municipal Corporation announced on 27th of February 2073. The area of this metropolitan city is 464.24 square kilometers. m. and the total population is 4, 14, 141.

Ilam

Ilam is the headquarters of Ilam district and the first municipality of the district in Koshi province in the eastern part of Nepal. This municipality is located in the Mahabharata mountain range. Being Nepal's largest producer of tea, its tea gardens are a major attraction among domestic and foreign tourists

Mustang

Mustang is the northernmost district in the Dhaulagiri zone of Western Development Zone of Nepal. Known as the district of Himalpari, this district is situated between two high peaks, Dhaulagiri and Nilgiri.

Himalayas

Annapurna Himalaya

Annapurna is a mountain located in the Annapurna range in the north-central Gandaki region of Nepal. It is the 10th highest mountain in the world at 8,091 meters above sea level and is known for the difficulty and danger of its climb.

Kanchenjunga Himalaya

Kanchenjunga Himal is the third highest mountain in the world located in Nepal. This mountain is 8,586 meters high. The Kanchenjunga Mountains are bounded by the Tamor River to the west, Lhonak Chu and Zhongsong La to the north, and Teesta River to the east. It is located on the border between India and Nepal.

Makalu

Makalu Himal is the fifth highest mountain in the world and the fourth highest mountain in Nepal. Its height is 8,463 meters. It is located in Sankhuwasabha district of Nepal. This mountain falls under the Kumbhakarna glacier range.
